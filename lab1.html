Mobile Development Topic: Mobile Banking

Definition

What is mobile backing, well according to James Check (2020) mobile banking is the act of making bank transactions on any mobile device. So, this means it can be anything from transferring your money around to the bank sending you notifications about your account.

History

For a long time starting back in 1967when ATM first set up and used (see Figure 1), part of the banking world has been trying to eliminate the need for some interaction with customers to increase transaction speed. Jump to early 1990s when online banking is taking off and we have the first instance on mobile baking according to Rezaul Hossain (2020) in the for of SMS banking notification. Then finally in early 2012 mobile banking as a whole hits the mainstream (see Figure 1) thanks to great advancements in the previous decade with mobile technology.

Advantages Uses

There are many advantages with mobile banking but most of them are also shared with online banking, such as not needing to go into a bank. But in an article by DBS (2022) about mobile banking the one advantages mobile banking has to all other forms is availability. Since in this day and age everyone has a mobile device with them in term of their phones, with mobile banking these become your bank. Making bill payments, checking you account activity, or paying back a friend for that pizza he bought can all be done with out even leaving your bed.
